Privacy Policy for DoDoLine

Last updated: June 28, 2026

DoDoLine ("the app", "we", "us") is a task-tracking app. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

1. Data stored on your device

All of your content — tasks, projects, milestones, and settings — is stored only on your device using local storage. We have no server and no user accounts, and this content is never transmitted to us or anyone else. If you uninstall the app, this data is removed with it.

2. Data we do not collect

We do not collect personal information. We do not use analytics or advertising, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. Purchases and subscriptions

DoDoLine offers an optional paid subscription ("Pro"). Purchases are processed by Google Play. To validate purchases and manage subscription status, we use RevenueCat, Inc. as our subscription infrastructure provider. When you make or restore a purchase, Google Play and RevenueCat may process purchase details and a device/app identifier for the sole purpose of managing your subscription. We do not receive your name, email address, or payment card details. Please refer to Google Play's and RevenueCat's privacy policies for how they handle this data.

4. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

5. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
